
# Zamzam Properties — Dubai Real Estate Website

## Overview
A premium, conversion-focused real estate website for a Dubai property agency. Modern design with gold/dark navy color scheme, mobile-first layout, and multiple lead capture touchpoints.

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Full-width background video placeholder (Dubai skyline) with overlay, bold headline ("Invest in Dubai Real Estate with High ROI & Golden Visa Eligibility"), two CTA buttons (Explore Properties, Book Free Consultation), and a compact quick contact form (name, email, phone, WhatsApp)
- **Featured Projects Carousel**: Top off-plan and ready properties with image slider, price range, payment plan summary, handover date, ROI estimate, and CTAs (View Details, Schedule a Tour)
- **Why Invest in Dubai**: Stats cards (ROI %, tax-free income, Golden Visa benefits), infrastructure highlights with icons
- **Testimonials Carousel**: Rotating reviews with buyer photos, quotes, and verified buyer badges
- **About Us Preview**: Brief intro with link to full About page

### 2. Properties Page
- **Advanced Search & Filters**: Budget range slider (AED), bedrooms selector, property type (Apartment, Villa, Commercial), location multi-select (Dubai Marina, Downtown, Business Bay, JLT, etc.)
- **Sorting**: Latest, Price low-to-high, Price high-to-low, ROI potential
- **Property Cards Grid**: Image slider, price, ROI %, bedrooms/bathrooms, short description, location tag, CTA button
- **Pagination** for browsing results

### 3. Property Detail Page
- **Image Gallery** with lightbox
- **Key Details**: Price, ROI, bedrooms, bathrooms, area (sq ft), property type, location
- **Payment Plan** breakdown
- **Floor Plan** section (placeholder images)
- **Handover Date & Developer** info
- **Schedule a Tour** form
- **Similar Properties** suggestions

### 4. About Us Page
- Agency story and mission
- Team section with photos, names, titles
- RERA license info display
- Partner/developer badges (Emaar, Sobha, Danube, etc.)

### 5. Contact Page
- Contact form with validation (name, email, phone, message, preferred contact method)
- Google Maps embed placeholder
- Office address, phone, WhatsApp, email display
- Business hours

---

## Global Conversion Elements
- **Sticky Header**: Logo, navigation, prominent "Schedule a Call" CTA button, always visible on scroll
- **WhatsApp Floating Button**: Bottom-right corner with pre-filled message
- **Exit-Intent Popup**: Triggers on mouse leave, offers free Dubai investment guide PDF download in exchange for email
- **AI Chatbot Widget**: Floating chat bubble for 24/7 instant responses (UI placeholder with sample responses)

---

## Footer
- Quick links (Home, Properties, About, Contact, Privacy Policy, Terms)
- Social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube)
- GDPR compliance notice
- Newsletter signup
- RERA license number

---

## Design Style
- **Color Palette**: Dark navy (#1a1f2e) + gold accents (#c8a45e) + white backgrounds for content sections
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large headlines, readable body text
- **Mobile-First**: Hamburger menu, stacked layouts, touch-friendly filters and buttons
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-ins on scroll, smooth hover effects on cards
- **All data is sample/mock data** — property listings, testimonials, and team info will use realistic placeholder content
